ABOUT

Space4 is an incubation, co-working and events space in Finsbury Park.

We host organisations and individuals who are engaged in using technology to build a fairer society. We particularly nurture young co-operatives who are dedicated to creating social value rather than shareholder profits.

Our residents work together to curate events, including talks, workshops, hackdays, screenings and more.

All profit generated from Space4 goes towards digital projects that make the world a fairer place.

Please note that unfortunately Space4 is not wheelchair accessible.

USE SPACE4

A Space For Working

Space4 includes 25 desks, which can be used on a short- or long-term basis.

Space4 is not your run-of-the mill serviced coworking space. Instead it is run cooperatively, meaning that everyone working here shares responsibility for making it a success. This includes promoting the space and the people here, organising and attending events and maintaining it as a great place to work.

  • £200+VAT per month per desk - reductions are possible in exchange for payment in kind
  • Broadband internet access - Up to 350Mb Download + 20Mb Upload
  • 24/7 access
  • Free use of meeting room (first come, first served booking)
  • Colour A4 + A3 printer
  • Projector available on request
  • Lockers available
  • Kitchen with microwave, kettle, crockery, cutlery etc.
  • Various social events with other residents and networks
  • We ask everyone who takes a desk to try to organise an event during their term here

A Space For Events

Our events room can host up to 50 people theatre style or standing and is also appropriate for smaller groups.
