Space4 is an incubation, co-working and events space in Finsbury Park.

Co-working

Space4 has 24 desk spaces, which can be used on a short- or long-term basis and hired by the day or by the month. Most users of the space work on projects that have some social value and which are related to tech and digital, though we’re welcoming to everyone! Our residents play an active role in making Space4 a positive place. See the community section for more details.

Desks cost in the region of £200+VAT/month or £20+VAT/day. Reductions are possible in exchange for payment in kind. Benefits include broadband internet access - up to 350Mb download + 20Mb upload, 24/7 access, use of our meeting and events spaces, printer, lockers, kitchen and access to our programme of educational and social events.

Contact us for a quote and for more details.

Room hire

We have two rooms available for hire.

Our boardroom can set up to 10 people round a large table. It comes equipped with projector and screen, plus two whiteboards. Flipcharts are available on request. Prices start from £20+VAT/hour.

Our events room can host up to 40 people theatre style or standing. It comes equipped with folding chairs and tables, a projector and screen and one whiteboards. Flipcharts are available on request. Prices start from £45+VAT/hour.

Both spaces are available to hire. More images are available on our Flickr page. Contact us for a quote and for more details.

Our co-working community

Space4 is more than just a serviced office. We are a community of people dedicated to making the world a fairer place, and to create a supportive working environment. The space is supported by CoTech, a UK-wide network of worker co-operatives in the digital tech sector. We have some of these co-ops working here, including AR designers Animorph, web developers Go Free Range, and open data specialists Open Data Services. We also have small charities, freelancers and small businesses, most of whom are using technology (to greater or lesser degrees!) to support their social aims.

Every Wednesday we eat lunch together in the space. This is an opportunity for people to get to know each other, swap ideas, share skills and invite others in. Users of the space are actively encouraged to participate in lunches and evening events, and even to host their own. Users are also invited to join our governance committee, which decides on future strategies for Space4. For users who aren’t in a position to pay the full price for a desk, we ask them to volunteer instead to help making the space function.
